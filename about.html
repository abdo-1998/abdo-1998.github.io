About us

I'm just a car fan that enjoys providing you with the best and latest car photography collection. I first started on Instagram by reposting only the best pictures taken by the best photographers, and I grew to more than 10,000 followers following that strategy but then I decided its time to start posting original content and photographs taken by myself. To show you my commitment to do so, every single picture on this website was shot and edited by me. I am always working on providing you with new original content never seen elsewhere before. Feel free to subscribe to my newsletter to find out when new photos are added.

Today, I have more than 20,000 active and engaging followers, I am posting the best automotive photography that I can take, and I am sharing more photos than ever.


Why I decided to post original content

During my Instagram journey, I noticed that other car/automotive pages are growing at a faster pace than I am, and that the only difference between the other pages and mine was the content. They never repost. All of their daily content is original and never seen else where before. So I set my goal to transform the entire page C//R to an original page, posting only original content, daily. Supercars are really rare where I'm are from, so I decided to contact supercar and automotive dealers and showrooms and ask them for persmission to take picture of the cars put on display. So far I have successfuly visited Audi, Aston Martin, Land Rover, Mercedes, Lamborghini, Bentley, McLaren, Ferrari, Porsche, Rolls Royce, Pagani, Maserati, BMW showrooms and more!
